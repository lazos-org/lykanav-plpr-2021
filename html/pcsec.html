Secure the computer

If you want to be secure your computer must be secure so in order to do this you can follow these steps:


1. Update your OS frequently as in most updates there are security fixes, which in case they are not applied the computer might be vulnerable to various attacks.

2. Enable a firewall in order to prevent connections malicious software may attemp to make.

3. Harden your browser

4. Use strong passwords to protect your device.
